Who am I?

I am an aspiring software developper and musician based in Montreal. I have spent my adult life performing, composing and studying music, and have spent the last while learning programming.

It has been a lot of fun learning different programming languages and building different projects. As someone who loves learning, getting into the tech world is amazing as there is so much to learn as things are constantly changing. There are so many inspiring people working in this field, and the mindset of constant growth is something I am excited to be a part of.
